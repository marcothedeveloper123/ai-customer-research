# Background {#background}

You want to design the "go to" and most used and most trusted personal assistant for consumers and get VCs to fund your new venture\!

To do so, you and your team need to know when people use AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini in their personal life, when they don't, and why.

## **Research Questions** {#research-questions}

Identify opportunity areas for innovation by understanding:

1. **Current behaviors**: How people use AI tools in their personal lives (e.g., planning, health, learning, productivity, decision-making)?

2. **Pain points and failures**: Where today‚Äôs tools break down or fall short of users‚Äô expectations?

3. **Mental models of trust and reliance**: What makes people trust an AI assistant? What are their fears or hesitations?

# Analysis Technique 2: Create a Research Summary Grid \- via Coloop {#analysis-technique-2:-create-a-research-summary-grid---via-coloop}

## **Detailed Instructions** {#detailed-instructions}

### **Create Research Summary Grid**

1. Log into Coloop.   
2. Make sure you are in AI for Customer Research \> 2025-07 Cohort.  
3. \[John completed\] ‚Äú+Create Project‚Äù,  
4. \[John completed\] Manage Project \> Setup Brief  
5. \[John completed\]  "Upload Interviews"  
6. \[John completed\] Each time you have to identify the interview moderator, and the research participant.  
7. Click on ‚ÄúAnalysis Grid‚Äù  
8. On the lower left click on the ‚Äú+‚Äù to create a new grid  
9. Name it with your name (e.g., John W)  
10. Add questions to your grid \- It will suggest ones that relate to your project objectives  
11. Review the general summary, and the differences between each individual.  
12. Continue the process for each question to create your research grid.  
13. Ask as many questions as you like.  
14. Jot down a few notes and be prepared to discuss in class with your classmates\!

### **Interrogate the Data**

1. In the left nave click on ‚ÄúAI chat‚Äù  
2. Ask your question of the data (perhaps the Research Questions above, plus some follow up).

# Analysis Technique 3: Interrogate the Data \- via Marvin {#analysis-technique-3:-interrogate-the-data---via-marvin}

## **Video Tutorial** {#video-tutorial-1}

[Video of me interrogating the data via Marvin](https://youtu.be/XpBocjg12S0)

## **Detailed Instructions** {#detailed-instructions-1}

### **Interrogate the data**

1. Log into Marvin.   
2. Make sure you are in the 2025-07 Cohort area.  
3. Click in the "Ask AI a question or Search project" form in the upper right.  
4. Click on "Advanced Search" on the bottom of the dropdown. This shows you all the options. You'll want to make sure our project is selected (which it should be by default). Note that you can also limit it to only certain tagged interviews, or limit the findings to more recent findings.  
5. Type in your question. This is likely to be one of the Research Challenge questions above.  
6. Click on "Ask AI" on the left side of the form field. This is the equivalent to hitting Submit.  
7. Jot down a few notes and be prepared to discuss in class with your classmates\!

### **Optional**

 In the left navigation go to "Deep Research". Try a few things like "Prove/Disprove a Hypothesis", "Find quotes about" and "Create an Insight Report"
