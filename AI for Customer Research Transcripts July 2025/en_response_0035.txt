MODERATOR: Tell me about a time you wanted to use AI for something but couldn't. What was the task? What prevented you from using AI?

PARTICIPANT: I've used AI to generate designs for me as a product designer. I provide the requirements, and it generates mock-ups for me. PARTICIPANT: Basically, I think it gives me a solution, and I tend to think more narrowly based on its solution rather than thinking broadly and coming up with a bunch of ideas on my own.
